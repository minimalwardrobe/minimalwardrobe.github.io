Simplify Your Wardrobe

The Minimalist Wardrobe was created out of a need to find simple clothing that has a practice purpose at an affordable price.

We’re not a luxury retail company. In fact we don’t make any of the clothes we recommend.

So then, what is this website?

It is an assortment of clothing found accross the internet that match with just about anything, most of it is machine washable, a lot of it is wrinkle free or water proof. It is easily maintainable and practical.

Do you make money?

We take an affiliate commission off of most of the items featured.
