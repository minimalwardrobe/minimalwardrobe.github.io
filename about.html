The Minimal Wardrobe was created out of a desire to find simple mens clothing that has a practice purpose at an affordable price.

What constitutes practical and useful?

All the clothing you find here is postively reviewed, solid colored, machine washable, and will typically have one or more of the following traits:

  • Afforable
  • Wrinkle free
  • Water proof / resistant
  • Stain resistant
